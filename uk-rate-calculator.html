  • Consignee email ID and Phone Number are mandatory for all shipments.
  • Address correction charges will be ₹1,250 + GST or ₹65/kg whichever is higher.
  • Address correction charges apply in the following cases:
    • Unable to deliver due to address problems
    • Spelling mistakes or missing words
    • Unable to contact consignee or incorrect contact details
    • Shipper or consignee gives instructions to change delivery address after dispatch
    • Given address on AWB is wrong or incomplete
